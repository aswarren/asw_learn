Brownies

Brownie
 
2 cups sugar
2 sticks butter
12 tablespoons cocoa
1 ½ cup plain flour
½ teaspoon salt
4 eggs
½ teaspoon vanilla extract
 
Mix sugar, butter, cocoa and heat on stove.  Take off heat and mix in remaining ingredients.  Bake @ 350 in a greased 9x13 pan for 22 to 25 minutes.  (Found it is best for a chewy brownie to cook less time and cake brownie cook longer .  I usually cook 23 minutes)
 
Icing
 
½ cup butter
3 tablespoons cocoa
6 tablespoons Coca-Cola
1 box powdered sugar
1 cup nuts (if desired)
 
Heat butter, cocoa and Coca-Cola until boils and remove from heat (I usually chop butter to ensure faster melting and remove from heat when boiling just begins), add sugar and nuts.  Put icing on brownies while icing is hot.
